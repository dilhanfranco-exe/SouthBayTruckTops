Tonneau Covers

​South Bay Truck Tops and Accessories is the only dealer of Camper Shells in the Los Angeles, South Bay area that is authorized to sell Snugtop and Leer Truck products.

LEER

Leer Tonneau covers retain the characteristic profile of pickup trucks, covering and securing the truck bed at the top of the cargo box. Leer covers are made of fiberglass and provide attractiveness, secure transportation, and overall security. Turning the truck bed into a huge lockable “trunk” enhances the finished look of the vehicle, and improves gas mileage by reducing aerodynamic drag. Our working displays help in the decision-making process and reassure the customers that they have found the right feel, look, and choice.

SnugTop

SnugTop’s Tonneau Covers feature double-lock security, high-strength construction, and easy opening and closing with the Lift Assist. SnugTop’s Tonneau Covers make for a great addition to any truck with the sleek, low-profile look and improved fuel economy. SnugTop is reliable because they provide extra care, attention to detail, and more durability. Here at South Bay Truck Tops we firmly stand behind SnugTop as it offers excentric design, innovation, and quality.
